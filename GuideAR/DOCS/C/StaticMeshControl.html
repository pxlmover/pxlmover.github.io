Mesh Control Setup:
In the Mesh Reaction rollout, add as many meshes you want, there is no limit. Easily configure Scale / Rotate / Translate using any combination your heart desires.
The strength multipliers can be adjusted to achieve the desired responsiveness on a per axis basis.
The Audio Track parameter will always look at the index of audio tracks UNLESS Band Mode is enabled, in which case it will look at the band number.
