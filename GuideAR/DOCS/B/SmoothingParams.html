Smooth Those Reactions:
Under the 'B-Audio' Rollout, you'll find the smoothing options for each audio track. Adjust smoothing parameters to control how quickly values respond to changes in your audio intensity.
Smoothing Distance - Larger Values = Smoother Response. Small Change Smoothing - How much smoothing to apply to small frequency changes. Large Change Smoothing - How much smoothing to apply to large frequency changes.
