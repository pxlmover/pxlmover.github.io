Smoothing Overview:
Under the 'B-Audio Rollout', you'll find the 'Smoothing Params' rollout. Adjust smoothing parameters to control how quickly values respond to changes in your audio signals.

This works PER TRACK

Smoothing Distance - Larger Values = Smoother Response.
Small Change Smoothing - How much smoothing to apply to small frequency changes.
Large Change Smoothing - How much smoothing to apply to large frequency changes.
